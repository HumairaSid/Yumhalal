The Ultimate **Gelatin-Free Marshmallow** Recipe: A Perfect Halal Treat üç¨

Posted by **Recipe Chef** on December 4, 2025 | Prep Time: 30 mins | Chill Time: 4 hours

Stop searching for certified snacks! Conventional marshmallows often contain non-halal gelatin, but we have the perfect solution. This recipe provides a delicious, detailed, and easy-to-follow guide for **homemade halal marshmallows** using the plant-based thickener, **Agar-Agar**.

Ingredients You'll Need

  • For the Agar-Agar Base:
  • 1/3 cup cold water
  • 2 tablespoons **Agar-Agar** powder (Crucial halal substitute)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • For the Syrup:
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light corn syrup or halal glucose syrup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. **Prepare the Pan:** Line a 9x13 inch pan with parchment paper and lightly dust with a mixture of powdered sugar and cornstarch.
  2. **Activate Agar:** In a saucepan, whisk together the cold water, **Agar-Agar** powder, and salt. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. **Make the Syrup:** In a separate pot, combine the sugar, corn syrup, and 1/2 cup water. Bring to a boil until the mixture reaches **$240^\circ\text{F}$** (Soft-Ball stage).
  4. **Combine:** Quickly heat the Agar-Agar mixture until boiling (about 1 minute). Pour the hot syrup into the bowl of a stand mixer. With the mixer running, slowly pour in the hot Agar-Agar mixture.
  5. **Whip:** Whip on high speed for 10-12 minutes until the mixture is thick, glossy, and holds stiff peaks. Add the vanilla during the last minute.
  6. **Set:** Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top. Let it cool and set completely at room temperature for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. **Cut and Dust:** Cut the sheet into cubes using a greased knife or pizza cutter. Toss the cubes thoroughly in the powdered sugar/cornstarch mixture to prevent sticking. Enjoy your **yummy halal treats**!
